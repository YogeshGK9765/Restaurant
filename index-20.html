Our Story

Where Every Meal Tells A Story

Founded in 2005, Spice Garden began as a small family kitchen in Pune with a single mission — to serve honest, soul-warming Indian food that tastes like home. Today, we proudly serve thousands of guests every month from our spacious, fully air-conditioned restaurant.

Our recipes have been passed down through three generations. Every dish is prepared fresh daily using hand-picked spices sourced directly from Kerala, Rajasthan, and the Deccan plateau.
